| # | Lesson Name | What You’ll Learn |

| 11 | [Winning the Endgame](11%20-%20Winning%20the%20Endgame) | • Why winning a won position is difficult <br> • How to convert material advantage correctly <br> • How to win by attacking in the endgame <br> • How to liquidate into winning pawn endings <br> • How to use zugzwang as a weapon <br> • How to defend against passed pawns |

| 13 | [Practical Defence](13%20-%20Practical%20Defence) | • How to survive when facing checkmate threats <br> • How to reduce or neutralize strong attacks <br> • How to exchange attacking pieces at the right moment <br> • How to bring defenders into the game effectively <br> • How to use counter-attacks as a defensive weapon <br> • How to defend against common tactical threats |
| 14 | [Mastering Rook Endings](14%20-%20Mastering%20Rook%20Endings) | • How to use rook and king activity effectively <br> • How to convert advantages in rook endings <br> • How to promote passed pawns correctly <br> • How to place the rook for maximum efficiency <br> • How to defend and draw difficult rook endings |
